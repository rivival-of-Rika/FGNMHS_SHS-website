About Us

As of February 2026, Francisco G. Nepomuceno Memorial High School (FGNMHS) is currently in its Early Registration period for the upcoming 2026–2027 school year, which runs from January 31 to February 27, 2026. During this window, incoming Grade 11 students are encouraged to pre-register at the school's help desk, which is open Monday through Friday and even on Saturdays to assist families. The campus is located at Citicenter, Brgy. Pandan, Angeles City, Philippines (2009), and serves as a major educational hub for the region.

The school's specialized offerings for the new academic year include both Academic Track programs (such as STEM, Business and Entrepreneurship, and Arts, Social Sciences, and Humanities) and Technical Professional Track programs (including Programming, Animation, Computer Systems Servicing, Events Management Services, and Kitchen Operations).

The Francisco G. Nepomuceno Memorial High School is a public school dedicated to offering quality education to students in Angeles City and surrounding areas. It works to develop students into responsible, skilled, and value-driven individuals ready for future challenges. With effective teaching, innovative programs, and strong community partnerships, the school nurtures the knowledge, skills, and talents of each student, guiding them toward success and a lifelong love of learning.

Mission

To provide reliable and high-quality services that meet the needs of our clients while promoting professionalism, teamwork, and continuous improvement.

Vision

To become a trusted and leading organization known for excellence, innovation, and positive contributions to society.
