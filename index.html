Welcome to the School of Choice

Where excellence is pursued, potential is unlocked, and futures are built preparing learners to lead and succeed in a changing world.

Francisco G. Nepomuceno Memorial High School Senior High School stands as a center of excellence dedicated to shaping competent, confident, and future-ready learners. Through rigorous academic programs and strand-based specializations, we equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed for higher education, employment, and entrepreneurship.

Beyond the classroom, we foster leadership, innovation, and character development in a supportive and inclusive learning environment. Guided by committed educators and a culture of excellence, we empower our students to discover their strengths, pursue their passions, and become responsible leaders of tomorrow.

Why choose us?

Quality Education

At Francisco G. Nepomuceno Memorial High School – Senior High School, we deliver quality education that prepares students for real-world success. Our strand-based programs are designed to strengthen academic excellence, critical thinking, and practical skills. Guided by dedicated and competent educators, we ensure that every learner receives meaningful instruction, strong mentorship, and opportunities to reach their full potential.

Excellent Facilities

We provide a learning environment equipped with functional classrooms, specialized laboratories, and modern learning resources that support academic and technical growth. Our facilities are designed to enhance hands-on learning, collaboration, and innovation—ensuring students gain practical experience alongside theoretical knowledge. At FGNMHS–Senior High School, the environment empowers students to learn comfortably, safely, and effectively.

Interesting School Life

School life at FGNMHS Senior High School goes beyond academics. We offer engaging activities, student organizations, leadership opportunities, and school events that promote creativity, teamwork, and personal growth. From academic competitions to cultural celebrations, students experience a vibrant and dynamic campus life that builds confidence, friendships, and unforgettable memories.
